Virtual keyboard for typing Modern Hebrew and transliterated Phoenician Script / Paleo-Hebrew.

To give an example of how this Modern-Paleo Hebrew transliteration works, the Modern Hebrew phrase "转 注专 拽" or "The Ancient Hebrew Script" would be mapped to Paleo-Hebrew like this:

转 (HaKetav) → (HKTB)
注专 (HaIvri) → (H'BRY)
拽 (HaKadum) → (HQDWM)

→ 转 注专 拽
